Steps for Encrypting/Decrypting Message.
1. Enter 'encode' for Encryption and 'decode' for Decryption.<Hit Enter>
2. After that type your message which you have to encrypt/Decrypt Ex:-Hello World.<Hit Enter>
3. Then type shift number which is basicaly main passcode Ex:-5.<Hit Enter>
4. You will get your encryted message just share it with receiver and also the shift number.
5. Vice versa for Decyption.
